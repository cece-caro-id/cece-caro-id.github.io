Annual Evaluations: Docusign Template Tutorial

By Cece Caro - Department of Film, Theatre, and Dance


Step 1: Log into Docusign with your SJSU account

The Annual Evaluation form is accessible in Docusign for SJSU employees and faculty. You must be logged in with your SJSU email account to access the form.

Personal email accounts will not have access to the template, per SJSU policy.

Go to docusign.com, select "Log In" in the top right hand corner of the screen, and type in your SJSU email. Your One SJSU authenticator will load. Once you are finished, continue to Step 2.

Step 2: Locate the Template

Select the "Template" tab at the top of the screen. Then select "All Templates" in the left side menu.

In the search box, copy/paste or begin typing "FT&D | Direct Observation of Teaching Form AY25/26".

Step 3: Select "Use"

Check to make sure the template title matches the title in Step 2. Make sure the form you are using has the correct AY year (AY 25/26).

Select blue button "Use" next to the file.

Step 4: Select Appropriate Recipients

In recipient box 1 "Reviewer/Observer" type in your name. Be sure the email address you use is your SJSU email.

Recipient box 2 is already filled with your depatment analyst. Continue to scroll down the page

In recipient box 3 "Faculty in Review" type in the faculty member you observed. Be sure their SJSU email is utilized

Recipient box 4 is prefilled with your Department Support coordinator. The faculty in review also recieves a copy (under the Department Coordinator box) and should auto populate

Select "Send".

Step 5: Send, Fill, and Sign

Once you send, Docusign will ask if you would like to sign now or later. Whenever you open the envelope to sign, this will be your opportunity to fill in all parts of the form assigned to you

For the top of the form, you will need:

  • Instructor First and Last Name
  • Course Name and Section Number you observed
  • Class Meeting pattern and mode
  • Evaluation Visit Date
  • Observation Duration
  • Student Attendance during your observation

If there is a section you do not have access to, you do not need to fill that space. Optional spaces are also indicated in the document.

Your signature is required on the first page.

When you are finished with the document, scroll to the bottom of the page and select "Finish"

That's it! You successfully completed the observation form. Once finished, the form will be send to the rest of the recipients for their part.
